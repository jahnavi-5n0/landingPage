Indians love for Jewellery is an open secret, they consider this is the best way to not just to accessorize but to build wealth too. Jewellery Box has a name for itself for the design, workmanship and for the traditional vibes theyusher.In India jewellery is often symbolized as larger than life aspect decoration- we often feel its presence in a very compelling way in weddings, in family rituals, in house warming ceremonies, in temples, in festivals and every other celebration. In Indian context jewellery is not seen merely as it holds a great auspicious significance. Jewellery is given as a token of Shagun in majority of the Indian celebrations. Coming from an Indian household each one of us have different memories associated with our Jewellery whether big or small. Over the years Indians diversified their adornment choices from gold to diamond, platinum and silver too.
